Research#

Dr McDermott is a researcher in Human-centred security. He seeks to answer questions relating to security, privacy and trust at the intersection between humans and digital technologies, in particular the Internet. Current Research interests include, but are not limited to:

Areas of Interest#

Human Factors in Cybersecurity:

  • Technical and psychological aspects of exploits against humans: insider threats, phishing and social engineering attacks
  • Behaviour-based cybersecurity
  • Cyber Situational Awareness
  • Usable Security

Cybersecurity, Privacy and Trust:

  • IoT Privacy and Security
  • Botnet and DDoS Detection
  • Digital footprints and user privacy
  • Awareness of privacy threats
  • Fake news (misinformation) and trustworthy content on social networks and the Internet

If you are interested in studying for a PhD in any of the topics above, please get in touch via my mailing list
